How to play the picture matching game!!! 

                1.Pairing: If the two pictures match, they are considered a match and will be removed from the game.
                
                2.Mismatch: If the two images do not match, both images will be closed and left as before.
                
                3.Winning the game: When all pairs of pictures are matched and removed from the game. The player wins the game.
                
                4.Exiting the game: Players can click on the "Exit" button to exit the game and return to the main page.
